Overview
Rust and Ruin is a dynamic roguelite game that blends intense action and strategic decision-making. Set in various planets from a future era of conflict. Players assume the role of an advanced mech unit that is sent on a recon mission to old abandoned planet that were colonised by various nations back when "United Earth" was still divided by borders and ideologies...
Features
  • Online Co-op (Up to 4 players)
  • Fight against various types of enemies and bosses
  • Explore procedurally generated maps, each run is different
  • Try different upgrades and weapons to find your playstyle
  • Complete challenges and earn rewards
  • Compete in leaderboards
  • Customize your mech with various patterns and colors
