Overview
Rust and Ruin is a dynamic roguelite game that blends intense action and strategic decision-making. Traverse through a gauntlet of runaway machinery, inspired by Synthetic and Helldivers 1, a swarm of rust seeking to test your steel, explore the environment of the old world that lies in ruin and reclaim what was lost. Upgrade your arsenal, challenge bosses and complete a variety of objectives in the name of reclamation. This is only a demo of what is to come.
Features
  • Online Co-op
  • Fight against various types of enemies and bosses
  • Explore procedurally generated maps, each run is different
  • Try different upgrades and weapons to find your playstyle
  • Complete challenges and earn rewards
  • Customize your mech with various patterns and colors
